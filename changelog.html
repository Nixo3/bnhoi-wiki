Changelog

This page documents all notable changes made to Balanced Never Heard Of It across its versions. BNHOI follows an iterative design philosophy focused on long-term progression, difficulty, and systemic depth.

BNHOI 1.3.0

by nixo · January 13, 2026

Additions

  • Added the Copper Man hostile mob.
  • Added copper nugget drops from the Copper Man.
  • Added drops to gravel blocks and introduced Flint Chips.
  • Added Radish as a new food item.
  • Added new survival difficulty mechanics:
    • Slowness when on low hunger and health.
    • Blindness at 2 hearts.
    • Chat announcements when players are near death.
  • Added Yellow Snow blocks and snowballs.

Changes

  • Replaced the iron ingot recipe with a saw-based recipe.
  • Changed the Stonecutter recipe.
  • Copper Man now spawns in groups of one.

The Copper Man is an orange-colored hostile entity that spawns at night. It attacks passive mobs and players, providing a generous amount of copper nuggets when defeated.

BNHOI 1.2.5 – Snowy Update

by nixo · December 30, 2025

  • Fixed multiple critical and progression-breaking issues.
  • Added Yellow Snow blocks and snowballs.
  • Wolves now produce Yellow Snow.
  • Fixed player phasing through blocks.
  • Leaves no longer randomly break without cause.

BNHOI 1.2.0

by nixo · December 22, 2025

Additions

  • Added Rough Wood.
  • Added Wooden Chunk.
  • Added Wooden Club.
  • Added Stone Pebble.
  • Added new advancements.

Changes

  • Added new Stonecutter recipe.
  • Replaced several textures.
  • Improved Wooden Club effectiveness for ore mining.
  • Added new recipes for cobblestone, slabs, and planks.
  • Logs no longer break from TNT or Creeper explosions.

BNHOI 1.1

by nixo · December 19, 2025

  • Structures within 1000 blocks of spawn now have significantly worse loot.
  • Added Plant Fibers as a drop from grass.
  • Sticks now break when mining copper ore.
  • Vanilla advancements are hidden to prioritize BNHOI advancements.
  • Reworked multiple textures.
  • Added new nuggets.
  • Added Diorite and Granite Powder.
  • Reworked the Brewing Stand progression.
  • Added Flint Block and Gravel Dust.

BNHOI 1.0

by nixo · December 19, 2025

  • Wood cannot be broken without a hatchet.
  • Stone cannot be broken without the correct tool.
  • Added the Mud Oven.
  • Dirt now drops Dirt Dust instead of dirt blocks.
  • Dirt Dust turns into mud when in contact with water.
  • Added copper nuggets.
  • Added Podzol Remainings.
  • Buffed zombie equipment and movement speed.
  • Added Yellow Creeper.
  • Passive mobs now fight back when attacked.
  • Trees have an increased chance of dropping sticks.
