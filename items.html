Items

This page lists all items added or significantly modified by Balanced Never Heard Of It. Items in BNHOI are designed to enforce slow progression, tool dependency, and material processing.

Primitive Items

Primitive items represent the earliest stage of survival and are often inefficient, fragile, or temporary.

  • Plant Fiber
  • Stone Pebble
  • Gravel Dust
  • Dirt Dust
  • Flint Chip

Wooden Items

Wooden items are unlocked after accessing basic wood processing and represent the first reliable tools and weapons.

  • Wooden Chunk
  • Wooden Club
  • Stick (modified behavior)

Tools

Tools in BNHOI are heavily tiered and often restricted in use. Using the wrong tool may result in no drops or item breakage. One of the main ones is the Copper Hatchet

Metal Items

  • Copper Nugget
  • Iron Nugget
  • Diorite Powder
  • Granite Powder

Food Items

  • Radish

Note: Most BNHOI items cannot be crafted using vanilla crafting tables and instead require specialized workstations or processing steps.
