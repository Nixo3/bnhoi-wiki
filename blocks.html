Blocks

Blocks in Balanced Never Heard Of It are designed to restrict early-game progress and encourage deliberate interaction with the environment.

Natural Blocks

  • Rough Wood
  • Flint Block
  • Gravel (modified drops)
  • Leaves (stable behavior)

Processed Blocks

  • Wood Planks (alternative acquisition)
  • Cobblestone (alternative recipe)
  • Cobblestone Slab (new recipe)

Functional Blocks

Functional blocks are required for progression and cannot be bypassed through vanilla mechanics.

  • Mud Oven
  • Stonecutter (modified recipe)
  • Brewing Stand (reworked process)

Snow Variants

  • Yellow Snow
  • Yellow Snowball

Note: Some blocks are immune to explosions, including logs, as part of BNHOI's world integrity mechanics.
